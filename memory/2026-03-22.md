### What Was Built

Complete admin panel for 青苗綜合醫療診所預約系統 with 6 management sections:

1. **🏥 門店管理** - Clinic branch management (2 branches: 青苗綜合醫療醫療診所 + 青苗中藥房)
2. **👨‍⚕️ 醫生管理** - Doctor management with avatar cards (~10 doctors: TCM, Physiotherapy, Bone Setting)
3. **💊 服務配置** - Service configuration (問診 15min, 治療 45min, 物理治療 60min, 中醫正骨 60min)
4. **🔗 醫生服務配置** - Doctor-service mapping
5. **📅 排班管理** - Schedule management with calendar view, batch copy, conflict detection
6. **📋 預約管理** - Appointment management with 3 views, search, sort, status toggle
